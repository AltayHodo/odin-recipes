Meatloaf

Description

This is the best meatloaf recipe you will ever find like ever. I never actually had meatloaf, I just thought of it because of the show Johnny Test.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon steak sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 3 tablespoons prepared mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine gorund beef, soup, chopped onion, rolled oats, eggs, steak sauce, ketchup and mustard in a large bowl. Mix well.
  3. Shape into loaf, and pat into a loaf pan
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
